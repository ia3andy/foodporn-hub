# 🍔 Double-Decker Burger Bliss — Stacked, Juicy, and "So Spicy"

> **"One bite and you're hooked. This burger doesn’t whisper — it moans."**

When it comes to guilty pleasures, few dishes hit like a stacked burger dripping with flavor. Towering patties, melted cheese, crispy bacon, and a secret sauce that should probably be illegal — this isn't just a burger. This is **foodporn royalty**.

---

## 🧨 The Stack That Attacks

Our burger starts with **two flame-grilled beef patties**, seared to perfection, with just enough pink in the middle to keep things juicy. Each patty is lovingly layered with **sharp cheddar**, oozing over the sides like a warm, cheesy hug.

Add **crispy bacon strips**, thin-sliced red onions, fresh lettuce, and a tomato that actually tastes like something. All cradled between a **buttery brioche bun** that's been toasted just enough to hold the chaos together.

## 🔥 The Sauce: “So Spicy”

We’re not playing around here. This isn’t ketchup-mayo cosplay.

Our signature sauce is a bold mix of:
- Chipotle aioli
- Roasted garlic
- A dash of smoked paprika
- A drop of honey (because sweet things should burn a little too)

It doesn’t just complement the burger — it owns it.
